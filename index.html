Thesis

Computer use is the next frontier.
Agents need somewhere to learn.

The race is on to automate the $3 trillion of work done on a computer each year. Models don't lack intelligence — what they lack is experience. Frist builds the training ground where models and agents can gain experience.

01 — Opportunity

A $3 trillion market.

The biggest economic prize in software since the browser. Every frontier lab is pointed at it.

02 — Bottleneck

Labs are environment-constrained.

The gap between "works on a benchmark" and "works in the wild" closes through environments, not parameters.

03 — Surface

Every site. Every app.

Browsers aren't enough. Agents need the full surface of a workday — web, desktop, and everything between.

Training

Training on the
synthetic internet.

An engine runs inside every twin — synthesizing tasks, executing rollouts, verifying rewards, streaming curated trajectories. The internet, rebuilt, turned into curriculum.

  1. 01

    Task synthesis

    Goals generated from environment structure — millions per site, graded by difficulty.

  3. 02

    Rollouts

    Agents attempt tasks inside twins, at training speed, thousands in parallel.

  5. 03

    Verifiers

    Deterministic rewards from twin state — no judges, no LLM-as-graders.

  7. 04

    Trajectories

    Action, state, reward — streamed, labeled, ready for on-policy RL or SFT.

Every captured site and app, running continuously — the synthetic internet, producing its own training data.

Throughput

Train on the internet.
Offline.

Because every twin runs offline and deterministically, two modes of training open up at volumes the live internet simply can't support.

On-policy RL

Training methods
human demos can't support.

Modern on-policy RL — GRPO and what comes next — demands rollout volumes no human pipeline can reach. Twins fork, run at training speed, and feed the loop directly.

Synthetic trajectories

1000× the volume of
human demonstrations.

When training needs video or screenshots, generate it in the twin — every action reproducible, every pixel labelable.
